Wikipedia now accepting BAT!

We're incredibly proud to announce that Wikipedia has joined the BAT/Brave ecosystem as a Verified Publisher. “We’re thrilled to welcome Wikipedia as a Brave Verified Publisher and to connect it directly to Brave users for automatic and anonymous [BAT] donations," said Brendan Eich, CEO and co-founder of Brave.

Brave Ads now on Android

Brave Ads is now live on Android, which means you can begin earning BAT for opting into privacy-respecting ads on your Android phone!

Users who consent to viewing privacy-preserving ads are rewarded with 70% of the ad revenue share in BAT.
